O2

March 2023

O2 is a poem I wrote in high school. I wanted to create a visualizer for it. I used plastacine clay and paper. The poem reads: "Our oxygen was cut short, limited to a single breath- then I saw you. We let go of the Carbon ties of the past. Inhale- we inhale together. Finally, we became O2."

Mr Pop's Red Flower

October 2023

On a walk by the river, Mr. Pop discovers a red flower! This was a project for my advanced stop motion class. I learned to operate the Dragon Frame Volo Motion Control Rig and incorporated flowing water effects and a walk cycle. The puppet was made from aluminum foil, wires, epoxy for reinforcement, and plasticine clay.
